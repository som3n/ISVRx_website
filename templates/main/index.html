Pioneering the Future of Space Propulsion with Semi-Cryogenic Technology

At the heart of ISVRx's propulsion system is our groundbreaking semi-cryogenic rocket engine, designed to revolutionize the space industry. Powered by liquid kerosene (RP-1) and liquid oxygen (LOX), this engine offers exceptional performance, increased efficiency, and cost-effectiveness compared to traditional cryogenic and solid-fuel engines.

Revolutionizing Drone Flight with Advanced Hydrogen Fuel Cells

ISVRx has developed an innovative hydrogen fuel cell for drones that extends flight time by up to 10 times compared to conventional battery-powered systems. This technology leverages the clean energy of hydrogen to provide drones with an efficient, sustainable power source.
